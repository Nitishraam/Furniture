Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before purchasing

What is the delivery time to Salem, Tamil Nadu?

Free delivery within 3-5 business days to Salem and surrounding areas. Larger items like sofas may take 5-7 days due to white-glove delivery service including room setup.

Is assembly required for this sofa?

No assembly required. Our sofas arrive fully assembled with professional white-glove delivery. We place it exactly where you want and remove all packaging materials.

How do I clean the Italian leather?

Simply wipe with a damp microfiber cloth and mild soap solution. Avoid harsh chemicals. For deeper cleaning, use leather conditioner every 6 months (included with purchase).

What is your return policy?

30-day hassle-free returns. Items must be in original condition with all packaging. Custom upholstery orders are final sale. Free return shipping within 100km of Salem.

Does it fit through standard doorways?

Yes, designed to fit through standard 32" doorways. Legs are removable for tight spaces. Our delivery team handles disassembly/reassembly if needed at no extra cost.
